Theme 04 Basic UX

04.02.01. Design sprint XD prototype 

For the design sprint we had to go through a long process in order to reach the final long term goal which was making another version of fronter which would be fully costumizable by the users. First we had to do our map with our long term goal, then we had to individually sketch our ideas , After we had to decide which ideas we should take from our sketches.

Moreover, we had to start building an XD prototype with adobe XD and the final step was testing our prototype with different users and writting down notes. We got a taste of how it is when you work as a professional UX designer in groups. and how important it is to sketch your ideas first before you decide to build a prototype.

04.04.01. Design sprint: Native app

So far, we had worked on developing a mobile solution of an improved Fronter implementing KEA’s goals and feature wishes. Later the teachers gave us full freedom to create whatever we thought would be the best app for kea's students. We went through a the same process again (map,sketch,decide, prototype,pitch) which gave us more knowledge about making decisions up front and making sketching before we move on etc.

We were given a few requirements like our native app has to be native for either an Android or iOS smartphone, our group size must be from 3 to 7 and we had to make them ourselves and we had to Make use of UI kits and implement them where needed in our native app prototype.

This theme was one of the best for groupworking, It said a lot about how challenging things can be when you work as a group because the other group members might not like the ideas you wanted to implement, so you have to take a step back and "take one for the team".

At the end of the week we had to Document and communicate the UX design solutions, like a professional. we had continuously, collected materials in a document (Google Doc) where we also have added descriptions, annotations and reflections on our work and the process.
